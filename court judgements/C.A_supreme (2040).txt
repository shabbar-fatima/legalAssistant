ORDER  
 Pursuant to the order of this Court dated 3 January 2024 the 
Election Commission of Pakistan has filed Cr iminal Misc. Application No. 
08/2024 on 6 January 2024 , comprising of 322 pages , and the Chief 
Secretary, Government of Punjab , has also filed Criminal  Misc. Application 
No. 10/2024 on 6 January 2024 , comprising of 242 pages. We enquired 
from the learned counsel representing the petitioner whether he wants to 
respond to the contents of the said CMAs and the learned counsel stated 
that he will be filing a  written response thereto and sought three days time 
to do so. Three days time is granted to do the needful whereafter the 
Election Commission of Pakistan and the Chief Secretary, Government of 
Punjab may consider the same. Adjourned to Monday, 15 January 2024 .
