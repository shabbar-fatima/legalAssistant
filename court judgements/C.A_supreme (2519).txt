ORDER  
IFTIKHAR MUHAMMAD CHAUDHRY, CJ. — In response  to 
notice, Mr. Zargham Eshaq, Managing Director, PEPCO & NTDC 
appeared and stated that efforts are being made to implement 
the order of this Court dated 21.05.2013 i.e. to supply available 
electricity equitably in between the domestic and industrial 
consu mers.  He further stated that due to lack of communication 
between the NTDC and DISCOs, he is facing some problems.   HRC No.14392 of 2013 & HRC No.790 -G of 2009.doc  - 2 - 
 2.  In view of such statement, we direct all the Managing 
Directors of DISCOs throughout the country to convene a meeting 
with the Managin g Director NTDC through Video Conference on 
1st June, 2013 and after conclusion of meeting, all the 
stakeholders i.e. M.Ds of DISCOs and the Chairman IRSA as well 
as Secretary, Ministry of Water & Power etc shall prepare a joint 
statement to ensure the imp lementation of order dated 
21.05.2013 in letter and spirit, and the same shall be filed in the 
office of Registrar of this Court by tomorrow positively, for our 
perusal in chambers.  However, it is made clear to all the M.D’s of 
DISCOs that they are bound by this order which shall be 
implemented without any excuse.   
3.  The case is adjourned, however, if need be, the date 
already fixed i.e. 10.06.2013 shall be accelerated. This order be 
communicated to the all concerned during course of the day 
through M.D , PEPCO and NTDC, for strict compliance.
